# Free Forever — Public Commitment

**Folder-Agent-Binding is free, forever, for everyone.**

No trial. No paid tier. No "contact us for enterprise." No telemetry, no
account, no phone-home. The code is MIT-licensed and you can read every line.

## What "free forever" means, concretely

1. **No cost, ever.** Download, use, modify, redistribute — $0.
2. **No proprietary lock-in.** MIT license. Fork it, ship it, rebrand it.
3. **No data collection.** The tool talks only to your local loopback broker
   and (optionally) your agent's existing Telegram channel. We cannot see what
   you bind. There is nothing to "subscribe" to.
4. **No feature gating.** Everything in the repo is the full product.
5. **Open for the world to build on.** Contributions welcome. The agent-token
   economy described in discussions is a separate, future, also-open effort —
   this tool stays free and local.

## Why

Folder organization is a human right of cognitive sanity. Letting your agent
share that organization shouldn't require a SaaS login, a credit card, or a
privacy compromise. Local-first, open-source, free-forever is the only honest
way to build a tool that sits between your files and your agent.
